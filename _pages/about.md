I am a Ph.D. Candidate (expected graduation: June 2026) in the joint program between **City University of Hong Kong** (Department of Information Systems) and **Xi'an Jiaotong University** (School of Management).

My research focuses on the **spillover effects of sharing economy**, particularly examining how bike-sharing systems influence various urban outcomes including home-sharing performance, urban crime, restaurant patronage, taxi markets, and traffic safety. I employ empirical methods and data analytics to understand the economic and social impacts of digital platforms in urban contexts.

**Research Interests:** Sharing Economy, Empirical Operations Management, Urban Computing
